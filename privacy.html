Privacy Policy

At PhishGuard, protecting user privacy and maintaining a safe browsing environment are among our top priorities. This website provides a tool that helps users analyze and check suspicious links to determine whether they may be associated with phishing or other malicious activity.

This Privacy Policy explains how information may be collected, used, and protected when users access this website.

Information We Collect

PhishGuard does not require users to create accounts or submit personal information in order to use the phishing link-checking tool.

However, certain non-personal information may automatically be collected when you visit the website, including:

  • Browser type and version
  • Device type and operating system
  • IP address (for security and analytics purposes)
  • Pages visited and time spent on the website
  • Referring websites or search engines

This information is used solely to improve the functionality, security, and performance of the website.

Submitted URLs for Analysis

When users enter a link into the phishing detection tool, the submitted URL may be processed by the system in order to analyze its security characteristics.

The system may evaluate factors such as domain reputation, URL structure, and potential phishing indicators. Submitted URLs are used solely for analysis and for improving the accuracy of the detection system.

Users are advised not to submit sensitive personal data through the analysis tool.

Cookies and Website Functionality

This website may use cookies and similar technologies to enhance the user experience, remember user preferences, and improve website performance.

Cookies are small data files stored on a user's device that help websites operate efficiently.

Users may choose to disable cookies through their browser settings; however, some website features may not function properly if cookies are disabled.

Google AdSense and Third-Party Advertising

This website uses third-party advertising services such as Google AdSense to display advertisements.

Google and other advertising partners may use cookies, web beacons, or similar technologies to collect anonymous information about user visits to this and other websites in order to provide more relevant advertisements.

These technologies may collect information such as:

  • IP address
  • Browser information
  • General geographic location
  • Browsing behavior and interaction with advertisements

Users may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google's Ad Settings page.

Third-Party Services

PhishGuard may use third-party services for analytics, website security, or performance monitoring. These services may collect anonymous statistical data to help improve the website and detect potential security threats.

These services operate under their own privacy policies.

External Links

This website may contain links to external websites or resources. PhishGuard is not responsible for the privacy practices, content, or security of third-party websites.

Users should review the privacy policies of any external sites they visit.

Website Security and Disclaimer

The phishing detection tool provided on this website is intended for educational and informational purposes only. While the system attempts to identify suspicious or malicious links, no automated system can guarantee 100% accuracy.

Users should always exercise caution when interacting with unknown links or websites.

Children’s Information

This website is not directed toward children under the age of 13. PhishGuard does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Policy Updates

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically to reflect changes in technology, legal requirements, or website functionality. Updates will be posted on this page.

Users are encouraged to review this policy periodically.
